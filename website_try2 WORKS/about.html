About the Project

This project focuses on gathering and analyzing data related to Green AI practices and technologies, specifically focusing on data gathered in August 2024.

The objective is to highlight sustainable approaches in AI, emphasizing energy efficiency, responsible data management, and eco-friendly deployment strategies.

The project also explores the latest advancements in AI that contribute to reducing the carbon footprint and promoting environmentally conscious technological development.

Key data points and findings from August 2024 have been used to provide insights into how AI practices are evolving and becoming more sustainable.
